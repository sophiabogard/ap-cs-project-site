Bridge Version 1.0

Watch our bridge!

The assignment was to create a bridge made out of cardboard that is efficient and durable enough to hold weight. The bridge had to be at least 300mm long with the max length being 400mm. It had to be wide enough to allow two toy cars to fit comfortably, and we could not use any materials besides carboard (single & double ply) and cardboard screws. We laser cut all of our designs, and use slots in connecting to avoid the use of screws. The bridge was 57g (0.057kg) and held a total of 45lbs (20.41kg) making the supported weight to weight ratio 358:1. What worked well in our bridge was the slot-on supports, and what failed was the tensile strength of the carboard, specifically on the sides. Improvements that can be made for version 2.0 is A-frames on the bridge for increased support.
